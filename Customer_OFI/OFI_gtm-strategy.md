### 3.3 Defensive Priorities (IEC 62443 Alignment)

- **Critical Processes (Pasteurization, Roasting):** SL 3 (Protect against sophisticated attacks)

- **Non-Safety (Warehouse, Logistics):** SL 1 (Protect against casual misuse)

**Foundational Requirements (IEC 62443-3-3):**
1. **Identification & Authentication Control (IAC):** Strong authentication for OT users, MFA
2. **Use Control (UC):** Least privilege for PLC modification, role-based access
3. **System Integrity (SI):** Firmware verification, code signing, antivirus where applicable
4. **Data Confidentiality (DC):** Encryption for formulas, customer data, farmer PII
5. **Restricted Data Flow (RDF):** Network segmentation, IT/OT DMZ, microsegmentation
6. **Timely Response to Events (TRE):** OT SIEM, 24/7 SOC, incident response plans
7. **Resource Availability (RA):** Redundancy for safety systems, backup/recovery
