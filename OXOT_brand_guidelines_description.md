1. LOGO SYSTEM
Primary Logo Construction
The OXOT logo consists of two primary elements:

Symbol (Left): A dynamic ribbon-style X-wing design featuring two interlocking ribbons

Red ribbon: Positioned in the foreground, creating depth and visual prominence
Blue ribbon: Positioned in the background, providing layered dimension
The ribbons cross at the center, forming a sophisticated X-shape with clear depth hierarchy
Logotype (Right): " geometric sans-serif typography

"OX" rendered in Red (#D60000)
"OT" rendered in Grey (#7C7C7C)
Bold, heavy weight with tight letter spacing
Sharp, angular letterforms with geometric precision
Logo Variations
Primary Logo: Full symbol + logotype (horizontal orientation)
Symbol Mark: Ribbon X-wing icon alone (for small applications or brand accents)
Dark Background Version: Full color logo on dark backgrounds (#1A1A1A)
Light Background Version: Full color logo on white or light backgrounds
Minimum Size Requirements
Full Logo: Minimum height of 16 units to maintain legibility
Symbol Mark: Minimum size ensures ribbon detail remains visible
Never scale below minimum specifications
Clear
Maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "O" in "OXOT"
No text, graphics, or other elements should intrude into this protected zone
2. COLOR PALETTE
Primary Colors
OXOT Red: #D60000

Usage: Primary brand color, "OX" text, red ribbon (foreground), accents, call-to-action elements
OXOT Blue: #0042D6

Usage: Secondary brand color, blue ribbon (background), primary line color, digital accents
Neutral Colors
Charcoal: #1A1A1A

Usage: Primary dark background, headers on light backgrounds
Grey: #7C7C7C

Usage: "OT" text, secondary text, subtle elements
White: #FFFFFF

Usage: Text on dark backgrounds, negative space, clean applications
Color Combinations
High contrast pairings are essential to the brand identity
Red and blue should never be used at equal visual weight in the same application
Maintain the established hierarchy: red (foreground), blue (background/accent)
3. TYPOGRAPHY
Primary Typeface
Font Family: Custom geometric sans-serif (bold/heavy weight)
Usage:
Logo "OXOT" text
Headlines and headers
Key messaging and taglines
Characteristics: Sharp angles, tight kerning, geometric precision, distinctive letterforms
Secondary Typeface
Font Family: Clean, professional sans-serif
Usage:
Body text
Descriptions and supporting copy
Technical specifications
Long-form content
Characteristics: Readable, elegant, professional
Typography Hierarchy
Headline Title: Bold, large scale, primary typeface
Subhead: Medium weight, secondary typeface
Body Text: Regular weight, secondary typeface, optimized for readability
Text Color Treatment
On dark backgrounds: White (#FFFFFF) or light grey (#E8E8E8)
On light backgrounds: Charcoal (#1A1A1A) or dark grey
Accent text: OXOT Red (#D60000) or OXOT Blue (#0042D6)

Technical Elegance: Balance precision with aesthetic refinement

6. APPLICATIONS
Business Collateral
Business Cards: Logo on background, contact information in white
Letterhead: Logo at top, subtle geometric accents, professional layout
Digital Applications: Responsive logo sizing, high-contrast presentation
Background Treatments
Primary: Dark backgrounds (#A1A1A to #0D0D0D) with subtle geometric patterns
Alternative: White or light backgrounds with full-color logo
Accent: Subtle 3D undulating patterns inspired by original brand inspiration
Usage Guidelines
Always maintain high contrast between logo and background
Use generous white space for sophisticated presentation
Apply consistent spacing and alignment across all materials
Maintain brand color hierarchy in all applications
7. DO'S AND DON'TS
DO:
✓ Use the approved logo files
✓ Maintain minimum size requirements
✓ Preserve clear space around logo
✓ Use specified brand colors
✓ Maintain high contrast
✓ Apply consistent typography
✓ Create sophisticated, magazine-quality layouts

DON'T:
✗ Alter logo proportions or colors
✗ Separate symbol from logotype without approval
✗ Use unapproved color combinations
✗ Place logo on busy or low-contrast backgrounds
✗ Rotate or distort the logo
✗ Recreate or redraw the logo
✗ Use low-resolution logo files

8. TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS
File Formats
Vector: AI, EPS, SVG (for scalable applications)
Raster: PNG (transparent background), JPG (for web)
Minimum resolution: 300 DPI for print applications
Color Modes
Digital/Screen: RGB
Print: CMYK (converted from RGB specifications)
Web: HEX codes as specified
